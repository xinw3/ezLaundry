ezLaundry Privacy Policy

Effective as of May 20, 2017

ezLaundry("We", "Us", "Our") has developed a mobile application("ezLaundry App", "the App") that stores your personal data in order to help you check status and make reservation of public laundry machines in your household. The purpose of this privacy policy is to inform you about the information we collect, how we use and the ways in which you can control the information. By using ezLaundry App, you accept the terms and conditions described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Receive or Collect from you

  • Registration Data: When you register, we ask you to provide certain information, which includes your name, email address, living address, and city, as well as a password for your account ("Registration Data").

  • Payment Information: If you choose to use the App to pay for your laundry fee, you may also be asked to provide your name and credit card information.("Payment Information"). We will pass through your Payment Information to the third party payment service and we don't store your Payment Information.

  • Technical Information: We collect your name, phone number, email address and other information you may provide when using the "Send Us Feedback" feature in the Settings view. When using that option, a "debugging.txt" file is automatically attached to the email body and contains contextual information (such as your username, device type, OS version, MAC address etc.) that help us address issues you have reported.

  • Contact Information: If you choose to participate in certain features through the App, you may provide us, and we may collect, your contact information, including your name, mailing address, and phone number ("Contact Information"). The email address you provided as part of your Registration Data is not considered Contact Information for the purposes of this policy.

  • Third Party Personal Information: There are certain features on the App that, should you choose to use them, will require you to provide personal information of your third party accout ("Third Party Personal Information").

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we receive or collect from you for the following purposes:

  • Provide you with the availability of laundry machines in your current address.

  • Identify the reservations under your user account.

  • Push notifications to your cellphone that you agree to receive.

  • Send your contact information to the laundry machine owner when you issue a maintenance ticket.

  • Develop and improve ezLaundry App based on your feedback and interactions.

  • Respond to your emails and provide you with support queries.

3. Your Options

  • You can choose not to receive any notifications from the App.

  • You can provide anonymous feedback by using an email account that does not reveal your identity, and does not include your name. You can also manually remove the "debugging.txt" attachment file from the email's body if you do not want to share such contextual information with us.

4. Disclosure to Third Parties

  • We pass your address to Google Map Geocoding API to get latitude and longitude of your address. With these parameters, we can provide you the availability of all laundry machines in your address.

  • If allowed, we may use your third party account information(Google, Facebook, Twitter) for authentication.

5. Manage Your Information

  • You can change your email address, password and address at any time.

  • If you would like to cancel your account, you can contact ezLaundry@gmail.com.

6. Security Safeguards

We are committed to securing your information and we take reasonable physical, organizational and technical measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, use, disclosure, modification or destruction.

These safeguards include, but are not limited to:

  • All transmition will be encrypted.

  • Storage on secure servers.

  • Encryption of your password and credit card information.

7. Children

We do not knowingly collect, use or disclose information from children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected information of a child under 13 without first receiving parental consent, we will take steps to delete such information within a reasonable time. If you have reason to believe that a child under the age of 13 has provided information to us, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information within a reasonable time.

8. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We will continue to evaluate this Privacy Policy at any time without notice. In case of substantial changes, we will alert you within 30 days before such changes become effective. By using ezLaundry App, you agree to be bound by the version of the Privacy Policy displayed on the date upon which you use the app. If you do not accept the amended or changed Privacy Policy, you may not access, use or continue to use the App. By continuing to access and use the App, you are acknowledging your acceptance of the amendments and changes. In any case, we recommend that you consult this page regularly in order to be informed of any changes in this Privacy Policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us by email at: ezLaundry@gmail.com. We will reply to all communications within reasonable time.
